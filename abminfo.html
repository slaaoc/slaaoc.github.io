2019 Annual Business Conference/Meeting Information

JULY 23-26, 2019
Sheraton Grand Sacramento
1230 J Street, 13th and J Street   Sacramento, CA 95814  USA 
Room questions or changes, call the F.W.S. Office 1-210-828-7900.  Please do not call the hotel.

ONLINE DELEGATE SIGNUp – Deadline March 25
See Raffle Info Sheet Here


Early Bird Registrations through May 9, 2019 receive a $50 discount.
Registration Deadline June 8, 2019, $75 late fee after June 8.
*If for any reason you will be registering a day or two late, please call the office as soon as possible.

Registration includes:

  • Three nights lodging, Check-in Tuesday through Check-out Friday  (Commuters and One-day Pass excluded)   You may add up to 3 nights before or 3 nights after the ABM at $125 per room night. Call the F.W.S. Office 1-210-828-7900, please do not call the hotel.
  • 10 Meals from lunch Tuesday through lunch Friday
    MENU TO BE POSTED      Diabetic, Gluten Free, and Vegetarian Food plans are available.  See Food section of General Information for details.      (One-day Pass receives food for that day)
    Meals are limited to Conference Members, Registered Observers, S.L.A.A. Staff, and Professional Service Providers to S.L.A.A.
  • Speaker meetings, Conference Activities, and Entertainment
    Auctions and events are open to all Members of the Conference, all Registered Observers, and all members of S.L.A.A. and their guests.
