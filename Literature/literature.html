Literature Information

Literature is a key resource of recovery and S.L.A.A. approved literature is available from a number of sources.


  • Click here for a free Newcomers' Packet.

  • You can order literature online direct from the Fellowship Wide Services (F.W.S.) web site. Click here for the F.W.S. Online Store.

 

  • You can purchase literature at the monthly Orange County Intergroup Meetings.  For best results please fill in a Literature Order Form before arriving. See the Intergroup page for the location, date and time of the next Orange County Intergroup meeting.
